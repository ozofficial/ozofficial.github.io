Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 15, 2025

OnlyEssential (“us”, “we”, or “our”) operates the Only Apps suite of mobile applications (the “Services”). This Privacy Policy explains our approach to privacy across all apps published under our developer account on Google Play.

Our Privacy Principles

  • No personal data collection. Our apps do not collect, store, or share personal data.
  • Works fully offline. Functionality is available without any network access.
  • No Internet permission. We do not request the INTERNET permission; apps cannot send or receive data online.
  • No ads. No trackers. No analytics SDKs.
  • Minimal permissions. When a permission is requested, it is strictly for on-device features and never used for tracking.

What Each App Does & Why Permissions Are Needed

Below are typical, on-device uses of permissions. Exact prompts may differ by Android version and OEM.

Log Data

We do not collect or store logs on our servers. Crash or usage analytics are not integrated.

Cookies & Trackers

Our Android apps do not use cookies or embed third-party trackers.

Security

Because our apps collect no personal data, request no Internet permission, and avoid third-party SDKs, there is no transmission of your information to us or to third parties. Your data remains on your device under your control.

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are general-audience utilities and do not knowingly collect information from children. As we collect no data, there is nothing to share with third parties.
