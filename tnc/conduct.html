Code of Conduct

Last updated: March 12, 2026

This Code of Conduct outlines the principles and standards followed by Quick Storage Cleaner and OZ Lab.

1. User Respect

We are committed to respecting users’ privacy, data, and overall experience.

2. Transparency

  • We clearly explain how app features work
  • We do not hide important functionality
  • We provide accurate information about app capabilities
  • Users remain in control of file management actions

3. Data Protection

  • We do not collect unnecessary personal information
  • File analysis is performed locally on the device whenever possible
  • No personal files are uploaded without user knowledge or consent
Protecting user privacy and maintaining transparency are important parts of our app design.

4. Responsible Permissions

Quick Storage Cleaner only requests permissions required for core app functionality, such as storage access, media access, or usage access features enabled by the user.

Permissions can be managed or revoked at any time through Android system settings.

5. Fair Advertising

  • Advertisements are displayed in a non-deceptive manner
  • We do not intentionally use misleading or intrusive ads
  • User experience and usability are respected

6. Safe Usage

  • Users are encouraged to review files before deletion
  • The app does not guarantee recovery of deleted data
  • The app does not guarantee device performance improvements

7. Continuous Improvement

We continuously improve Quick Storage Cleaner to enhance usability, stability, security, and overall user experience.

8. Policy Compliance

We aim to follow Google Play policies and applicable laws related to user privacy, advertising, and app functionality.

Email: onlyapp.contact@gmail.com

10. Developer Information
