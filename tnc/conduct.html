Code of Conduct

Last updated: March 12, 2026

This Code of Conduct outlines the principles and standards followed by Cleaner.

---

1. User Respect

We are committed to respecting users' privacy, data, and overall experience.

---

2. Transparency

  • We clearly explain how the app works
  • We do not hide important functionality
  • We provide honest information about features
---

3. Data Protection

  • We do not collect unnecessary personal data
  • All file analysis is performed locally on the device
  • No personal files are uploaded without user consent
---

4. Responsible Permissions

We only request permissions that are required for core app functionality, such as storage access and usage data.

---

5. Fair Advertising

  • Ads are displayed in a non-deceptive manner
  • We do not use misleading or intrusive ads
  • User experience is respected
---

6. Safe Usage

  • Users are encouraged to review files before deleting
  • We do not guarantee recovery of deleted data
---

7. Continuous Improvement

We continuously improve the app to enhance performance, security, and usability.

---

8. Policy Compliance

We follow Google Play policies and applicable laws.
