Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the following applications developed under the Neu brand:

  • Neu Phone (Dialer)
  • Neu Messages
  • Neu Contacts

1. Information We Collect

Our apps may access the following data strictly for core functionality:

  • Call logs – to display and manage call history (Neu Phone)
  • Contacts – to display names, manage contacts, and enable calling (All apps)
  • SMS/Messages – to send, receive, and manage messages (Neu Messages)
  • Phone state – to handle incoming and outgoing calls

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data only to provide essential app features:

  • Dialing and call management
  • Managing contacts
  • Sending and receiving SMS messages
  • Displaying caller and contact information
  • Improving app performance and stability

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Data Storage

All data is processed locally on your device. We do not store personal data on external servers.

5. Permissions

Our apps may request the following permissions:

  • READ_CALL_LOG / WRITE_CALL_LOG
  • READ_CONTACTS / WRITE_CONTACTS
  • SEND_SMS / RECEIVE_SMS / READ_SMS
  • CALL_PHONE

These permissions are used only for core functionality of the apps.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data and ensure it is not misused.

7. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
