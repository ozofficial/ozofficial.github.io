General

Only Phone ("the App") is developed by OZ Lab.

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used, and disclosed.

By using Only Phone, you agree that your personal information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Core Privacy Principle: Your call history, contacts, phone numbers, and dialer-related data are processed exclusively on your device (on-device). No personal call data or contact information is transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

1.1. Device Phone Number - Local Display

Data Accessed: Your mobile phone number(s) associated with your device's SIM card(s).

Purpose: To display SIM information inside the app and allow convenient SIM selection for phone calls.

Processing: This information is processed locally on your device only. Your phone number is never uploaded or stored on our servers.

Permission: READ_PHONE_STATE (optional but required for multi-SIM support).

1.2. Call Logs and Dialer Functionality

Data Accessed: Call history, incoming/outgoing phone numbers, and call timestamps.

Purpose: To provide core dialer functionality such as displaying recent calls, identifying callers, and managing call history.

Processing: All call log processing happens locally on your device. Call history and phone numbers are never transmitted to external servers.

Permission: READ_CALL_LOG and WRITE_CALL_LOG (required for call management features).

1.3. Contacts (Optional Functionality)

Data Accessed: Contact names and phone numbers stored in your address book.

Purpose: To display contact names, caller information, and contact suggestions inside the dialer interface.

Processing: Contact data is accessed locally only and never leaves your device.

Permission: READ_CONTACTS and WRITE_CONTACTS (optional).

1.4. Clipboard Features

Data: Phone numbers copied using app features.

Note: Copied data is placed into your device’s system clipboard, which may be accessible by other apps depending on your Android version and device settings.

1.5. Internet Access Usage

The App may require internet access only for:

  • Processing in-app purchases through Google Play Billing.
  • Optional app updates or support-related functionality.

These operations do not involve transmitting your private call logs, contacts, or phone numbers.

2. Data Storage and Security

Local-Only Processing: Personal data such as contacts, call logs, and phone numbers are processed and stored only on your device.

No Cloud Storage: We do not operate servers that store your private call history or contact information.

Device Security: The security of your information depends on your device’s own security protections. We recommend keeping your device updated.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use:

  • Google Play Billing for in-app purchases.

The App does not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics services that collect personal dialer data.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
