General

Only Gallery ("the App") is developed by OZ Lab.

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used, and disclosed.

By using Only Gallery, you agree that your information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Core Privacy Principle: Your photos, videos, albums, and media files are processed exclusively on your device (on-device). No personal media content is uploaded, transmitted, or shared with external servers.

1. Information Collection and Use

Only Gallery prioritizes the protection of your privacy and does not engage in the collection of personal information during your use of the App.

The App operates primarily offline, and your media files remain stored locally on your device.

To provide gallery functionality, the App may access:

  • Photos and images
  • Videos and media files
  • Albums and folders
  • File metadata such as file names and timestamps

This information is processed locally only and is never uploaded or shared with third parties.

2. Permissions

The App may request storage or media access permissions solely to:

  • Display photos and videos
  • Create, edit, move, or delete media files
  • Organize albums and folders
  • Enable media sharing initiated by the user

These permissions are used only for core gallery functionality.

3. Log Data

Only Gallery does not collect log data, analytics, or usage tracking information.

Our commitment to privacy includes the non-collection of personal or technical information while you use the App.

4. Cookies

Only Gallery does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

The App does not store tracking information on your device.

5. Security

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy.

Since Only Gallery does not upload or transmit personal media files, there are no cloud-related risks involving your private photos or videos.

The security of your data depends on your device’s built-in security protections. We recommend keeping your device updated and protected with a screen lock.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use:

  • Google Play Billing for optional in-app purchases

The App does not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics services that collect personal gallery data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
