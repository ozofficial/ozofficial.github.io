General

Only Calendar ("the App") is developed by OZ Lab.

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used, and disclosed.

By using Only Calendar, you agree that your information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Core Privacy Principle: Your calendar events, reminders, schedules, and related information are processed exclusively on your device (on-device). No calendar data is uploaded, transmitted, or shared with external servers by the App.

1. Information Collection and Use

Only Calendar is designed with privacy in mind and does not collect personal information during normal app usage.

To provide calendar functionality, the App may access:

  • Calendar events and reminders
  • Event titles, dates, times, and descriptions
  • Calendar account information available on your device
  • Notification and reminder settings

All calendar data is processed locally on your device only.

The App does not upload, transmit, or store your calendar events on external servers.

2. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • READ_CALENDAR – Required to display calendar events
  • WRITE_CALENDAR – Required to create, edit, and delete events
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Required for reminders and event notifications

These permissions are used solely for core calendar functionality.

3. Notifications and Reminders

Reminder notifications are generated locally on your device.

Event reminders and notification schedules are not transmitted externally.

4. Log Data

Only Calendar does not collect analytics, usage statistics, or personal log data.

No calendar content or scheduling information is transmitted externally.

5. Cookies

Only Calendar does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

The App does not create advertising identifiers or behavioral tracking profiles.

6. Security

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy.

Since calendar data remains on your device, you maintain control over your schedules and reminders.

We recommend using your device’s security features, including screen lock and updated system software, for additional protection.

7. Third-Party Services

The App may use:

  • Google Play Billing for optional in-app purchases

The App does not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics services that collect calendar-related information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
