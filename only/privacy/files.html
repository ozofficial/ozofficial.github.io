General

Only Files ("the App") is developed by OZ Lab.

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used, and disclosed.

By using Only Files, you agree that your information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Core Privacy Principle: Your files, folders, documents, images, videos, and storage-related information are processed exclusively on your device (on-device). No personal file content is uploaded, transmitted, or shared with external servers.

1. Information Collection and Use

Only Files is designed with privacy in mind and does not collect personal information from users during normal app usage.

The App operates locally on your device and provides file management functionality without transmitting your files to external servers.

To provide core functionality, the App may access:

  • Files and folders stored on your device
  • Documents, images, videos, audio files, and downloads
  • File names, sizes, timestamps, and metadata
  • Storage information and available device space

This information is processed locally only and remains on your device.

2. Permissions

The App may request storage-related permissions solely to:

  • Browse and display files
  • Create, rename, move, copy, or delete files and folders
  • Organize device storage
  • Allow user-initiated file sharing
  • Support file search and sorting features

These permissions are used only for file management functionality.

3. Clipboard Features

The App may use your device clipboard when copying file names, file paths, or text selected by the user.

Clipboard content may be accessible to other applications depending on your Android version and device settings.

4. Log Data

Only Files does not collect analytics, usage statistics, or personal log data.

No file content or storage information is transmitted externally.

5. Cookies

Only Files does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

The App does not create advertising identifiers or behavioral tracking profiles.

6. Security

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy.

Since Only Files does not upload or store your files on external servers, your personal files remain under your control on your device.

We recommend using your device’s built-in security features such as screen lock and encryption for additional protection.

7. Third-Party Services

The App may use:

  • Google Play Billing for optional in-app purchases

The App does not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics services that collect file-related information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
