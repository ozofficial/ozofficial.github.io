General

Only Contacts ("the App") is developed by OZ Lab.

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used, and disclosed.

By using Only Contacts, you agree that your personal information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Core Privacy Principle: Your contacts, phone numbers, email addresses, and related contact information are processed exclusively on your device (on-device). No contact data is transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

1.1. Contacts Access

Data Accessed: Contact names, phone numbers, email addresses, profile photos, and other information stored in your device’s contact list.

Purpose: To display, create, edit, organize, search, and manage contacts within the app.

Processing: All contact data is processed locally on your device only. Your contacts are never uploaded, shared, or stored on our servers.

Permission: READ_CONTACTS and WRITE_CONTACTS (required for contact management features).

1.2. Phone and SIM Information

Data Accessed: Device phone number(s) and SIM-related information.

Purpose: To support contact actions such as calling, messaging, or assigning SIM preferences where supported.

Processing: This information remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Permission: READ_PHONE_STATE (optional).

1.3. Clipboard Features

Data: Contact details or phone numbers copied using app features.

Note: Copied information is stored in your device’s system clipboard, which may be accessible by other applications depending on your Android version and device settings.

1.4. Internet Access Usage

The App may require internet access only for:

  • Processing in-app purchases through Google Play Billing.
  • Optional support or update-related functionality.

These operations do not involve transmitting your private contact data.

2. Data Storage and Security

Local-Only Processing: All contact data is stored and processed only on your device.

No Cloud Storage: We do not operate servers that store your contacts or personal address book information.

Device Security: The security of your data depends on your device’s own security protections. We recommend keeping your operating system updated.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use:

  • Google Play Billing for in-app purchases.

The App does not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics services that collect personal contact information.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
