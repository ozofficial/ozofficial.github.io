General

Only Voice Recorder ("the App") is developed by OZ Lab.

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used, and disclosed.

By using Only Voice Recorder, you agree that your information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Core Privacy Principle: Your audio recordings, voice notes, and recording-related data are processed exclusively on your device (on-device). No audio recordings are uploaded, transmitted, or shared with external servers.

1. Information Collection and Use

Only Voice Recorder is designed to protect your privacy and does not collect personal information during normal app usage.

The App operates locally on your device, and your recordings remain stored only on your device unless you explicitly choose to share them.

To provide recording functionality, the App may access:

  • Microphone input for audio recording
  • Audio files created within the App
  • File names, durations, and timestamps
  • Storage information required to save recordings

All processing occurs locally on your device.

2. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • RECORD_AUDIO – Required to record voice and audio
  • Storage/Media Access – Required to save, manage, and share recordings

These permissions are used solely for core voice recording functionality.

3. Clipboard and Sharing Features

The App may use clipboard or file-sharing features only when initiated by the user, such as copying recording names or sharing audio files.

Shared files are handled through standard Android sharing mechanisms.

4. Log Data

Only Voice Recorder does not collect analytics, usage statistics, or personal log data.

No audio content or recording information is transmitted externally.

5. Cookies

Only Voice Recorder does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

The App does not create advertising identifiers or behavioral tracking profiles.

6. Security

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy.

Since recordings are stored locally on your device, you maintain full control over your audio files.

We recommend protecting your device with screen lock, encryption, and updated system software for additional security.

7. Third-Party Services

The App may use:

  • Google Play Billing for optional in-app purchases

The App does not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics services that collect recording-related information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
