Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the application (“App”, “Service”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use the mobile application.

Information Collection and Use

We value your privacy and aim to collect as little information as possible.

The App itself does not directly collect or store personal information unless required for specific features. Depending on the functionality you use, limited information such as your email address or phone number may be processed locally on your device to enable app features.

Most app functionality works offline, and your data generally remains on your device.

Third-Party Services

The App may use trusted third-party services that can collect information used to identify you. These services may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These third-party services may collect information such as:

  • Device information
  • App interactions
  • Crash logs
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Purchase information

Please refer to the respective privacy policies of these providers for more information.

Log Data

In case of an app error, third-party services may collect technical information such as:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • App configuration
  • Time and date of use
  • Crash diagnostics

This information is used only to improve app stability and performance.

Cookies

The App itself does not use cookies directly. However, third-party SDKs or advertising services may use similar technologies to improve their services and provide relevant content or advertisements.

Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
