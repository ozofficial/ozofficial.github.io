General

Only Apps ("the Apps", "Services", "we", "our", or "us") are developed by OZ Lab.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our applications, including but not limited to:

  • Only Phone
  • Only Messages
  • Only Contacts
  • Only Gallery
  • Only Files
  • Only Voice Recorder
  • Only Calendar

By using our applications, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

Core Privacy Principle: Your personal data, including contacts, messages, call logs, media files, recordings, calendar events, and documents, is processed primarily on your device (on-device). We do not upload or sell your private content to external servers.

1. Information and Permissions

Depending on the application and features used, our apps may request access to:

  • Phone numbers and SIM information
  • Call logs and dialer functionality
  • SMS/MMS messages
  • Contacts and address book data
  • Photos, videos, and media files
  • Documents and storage files
  • Microphone/audio recordings
  • Calendar events and reminders
  • Clipboard data initiated by the user

These permissions are used solely to provide the core functionality of the respective applications.

Data accessed by the apps is generally processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Local-Only Processing

Our applications are designed with privacy in mind.

Personal content such as messages, contacts, recordings, files, photos, videos, and calendar events remains stored on your device.

We do not operate cloud servers that store your personal app content.

3. Clipboard Features

Some apps may use clipboard functionality to copy phone numbers, OTP codes, file names, or other user-selected content.

Clipboard data is managed by your device’s operating system and may be accessible to other applications depending on Android version and device settings.

4. Internet Access

Certain apps may require internet access for limited functionality such as:

  • Sending or receiving MMS messages
  • Google Play Billing and in-app purchases
  • Optional support or update-related features

These operations do not involve uploading your private personal content to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Some applications may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Play Services

Unless explicitly stated otherwise, our apps do not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics services that collect personal user content.

6. Log Data

We generally do not collect personal usage logs, analytics data, or behavioral tracking information.

Technical crash information may be generated by the Android operating system or Google Play services for app stability purposes.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our apps do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

We do not create advertising profiles or track user behavior across apps.

8. Security

We value your trust and strive to protect your privacy.

Since your data remains primarily on your device, you maintain control over your personal information.

We recommend using device security features such as screen lock, encryption, and updated system software.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our applications are not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
