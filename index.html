About

OnlyZero brings together your most important Android experiences — calling, messaging, photos, music, calendar, and more — into one unified hub designed for simplicity, speed, and clarity. Every feature is crafted to help you stay connected, productive, and present — without distractions. From managing your contacts to capturing moments, OnlyZero keeps the core of your digital life organised and beautifully accessible. It’s not about having more apps — it’s about having the right ones, working together seamlessly. Experience the balance of minimalism and utility — experience OnlyZero.
